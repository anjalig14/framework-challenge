Hugo Ramirez

Composer

Ramirez is a multi-genre composer based in New York City. Growing up in Madrid, Spain
he grew up around music as his family were big musicians themselves. Ramirez has been
composing music for over 15 years creating beautiful and unique pieces in various genres
including classical, contemporary, and even folklor Flamenco. He strives to create emotional
depth in his pieces and a sense of emotional connection with his audience worldwide.
